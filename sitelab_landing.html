Grid Landslide Resilience

Calculates Landslide Risk Index (LRI) around pylons using slope angles and dynamic vegetative density index (NDVI) parsed from Copernicus Sentinel-2 L2A tiles.

Solana Swarm Oracles

An autonomous multi-agent swarm debates the landslide hazard level. Upon verification, the Treasurer agent signs and broadcasts onchain escrow releases directly on Solana Devnet.

0xBow Privacy Pools v2

Critical infrastructure locations are highly sensitive. Privacy Pools v2 allows utility operators to fund segment escrows anonymously using zero-knowledge membership proofs.

Blockchain for Good Alliance

Delivering social and environmental impact. NAura bridges ecology rewards and utility operations, paying local reforestation teams to stabilize vulnerable hills.

The Autonomous Alert & Release Pipeline

NAura eliminates manual audits. When heavy rains degrade a slope, the AI detects the risk and releases funds within seconds to dispatch geotextile stabilization units.
