How it works, in three steps

Simple enough for anyone. Strong enough to trust.

1. Money is set aside

Funds for a forest are locked safely in advance — kept aside until the work is proven, not before.

2. Satellites watch from space

Every few weeks, satellites measure how green and healthy the land has become. Machines check the photos — no one can fake it.

3. Real growth gets paid

When the trees genuinely grow back, the money is released to the people doing the planting. Proof first, payment second.
